About this App

Faith Journey DGroup
Created by KC. Powered by AI-assisted reflection.

Faith and care note: This app supports prayer, journaling, Gospel follow-up, and discipleship monitoring in a way that honors God and respects personal privacy. AI reflections are encouragement tools only. They are not direct words from God, professional counseling, medical advice, legal advice, or emergency support.

Leader / Member Design

Mother Account: DGroup leader sees group dashboard, Gospel pipeline, follow-up alerts, shared prayer requests, and shared testimonies.

Member Account: Each member owns their private diary and may choose what to share with the group.

Privacy rule: No leader should read private diary entries unless the member intentionally shares them.

Privacy & Data

Prototype data is stored on this device/browser only. For real mother/member accounts, cloud sync, and group dashboard, connect Firebase or Supabase with secure authentication and role-based access.
